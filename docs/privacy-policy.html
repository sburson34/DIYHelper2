DIY Helper Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Introduction

DIY Helper ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact information: Name, email address, and phone number (when you choose to request professional help).
  • Location data: Zip code (entered manually for local store searches, weather forecasts, and permit information).
  • Photos and videos: Images and videos you capture of home repair projects for AI analysis.
  • Voice recordings: Audio captured when using voice description or hands-free workshop mode, converted to text on your device.
  • Project descriptions: Text descriptions of home repair issues you enter.
  • Tool inventory: List of tools and materials you add to track what you own.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data and error reports via Sentry to help us fix bugs. No personal information is included in crash reports.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version (included in crash reports only).

How We Use Your Information

  • AI analysis: Photos and descriptions are sent to our server and processed using OpenAI's GPT-4o to generate repair guides. Images are not stored on our servers after processing.
  • Professional help requests: When you request professional help, your contact information and project details are shared with home repair professionals.
  • Community library: If you opt in, completed project guides (without personal information) may be shared anonymously in the community library.
  • App improvement: Anonymous crash reports help us identify and fix bugs.

Data Storage

  • Local storage: Most of your data (projects, tools, preferences, profile) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • Server processing: Photos and descriptions sent for AI analysis are transmitted to our backend server, forwarded to OpenAI for processing, and are not permanently stored.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

  • OpenAI: Project photos and descriptions are sent to OpenAI for AI-powered analysis. OpenAI's data usage is governed by their API data usage policy.
  • Professional help: Your contact info is shared with professionals only when you explicitly request help.
  • Community: Anonymized project guides are shared only if you opt in.
  • Crash reporting: Anonymous crash data is sent to Sentry for bug tracking.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete your data: You can delete all locally stored data at any time from Settings > Delete My Data.
  • Community opt-out: You can disable community sharing at any time in Settings.
  • Notifications: You can enable or disable project reminders in Settings.
  • Camera and microphone: You can revoke camera, microphone, and speech recognition permissions at any time through your device's system settings.

Children's Privacy

DIY Helper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between the app and our servers. Personal data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app.
