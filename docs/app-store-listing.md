## Full Description (Both Stores)

Turn your phone into a home repair expert. DIY Helper uses AI to analyze photos of your repair issues and generates clear, step-by-step guides to fix them yourself.

**How it works:**
1. Take photos of the problem
2. Describe what's going on (type or use your voice)
3. Get a complete repair guide with steps, tools, cost estimates, and safety tips

**Hands-Free Workshop Mode**
Working with your hands? Turn on voice control. DIY Helper reads each step aloud and listens for your commands — say "done" to move to the next step, or "Hey Helper" to ask questions without touching your phone.

**Smart Tool & Shopping Management**
- Track the tools you already own
- Get automatic shopping lists that skip items you have
- Scan barcodes at the store to check off items

**Know When to DIY and When to Call a Pro**
- AI diagnostics help identify the problem before you start
- Clear guidance on when a repair needs a licensed professional
- Request quotes from pros directly in the app
- See how repairs impact your home's value

**Stay Safe**
- Safety tips customized to your project
- Chemical hazard warnings from official databases
- Emergency quick-reference for leaks, electrical hazards, gas, and fire

**More Features:**
- Paint color matching from photos
- Photo annotation to mark problem areas
- Export guides as PDF for offline use
- Community library of shared repair guides
- English and Spanish language support
- Dark mode

Whether it's a leaky faucet or a deck rebuild, DIY Helper gives you the confidence to tackle it.
